FAQ's
What is Future Bazaar wallet?
It's like the wallet in your pocket, but virtual. More formally, its a pre-paid credit system for a hassle free shopping experience. Wallet makes online purchases a Joy. No more credit card hassles or bank website failures.
How can I use my wallet?
You can pay once and shop multiple times using your wallet cash. Shopping becomes simpler, smoother and faster.
If you happen to cancel an order, we will refund the money to your wallet. You can also withdraw your refund by contacting our customer service.
Is my money safe?
Future Bazaar wallet is highly secure and only you can operate your it. Do not share your PIN with anyone and change it frequently.
